# confirm.md — SikaBoafo Product Confirmations

## 🧠 Core Idea

- App must be simple, fast, and easy to use
- Built for real-life busy Ghanaian merchants
- Speed matters more than perfect accuracy

---

## 🏪 How the System Works

- Track **stock (what you have)**
- Track **sales (what you sold)**
- Track **payments/debts (how you got paid)**

---

## 📦 Inventory (Stock)

- Merchant adds products when they buy from wholesale
- Merchant can restock anytime
- Stock increases when buying, decreases when selling

---

## 💰 Sales (IMPORTANT)

- No complicated sales form
- Sales happen by **reducing stock**

👉 User taps `-1`, `-5`, etc.
👉 System automatically records a sale in the background

---

## ⚡ Speed Rule

- Each action should take **less than 1 second**
- No typing during busy selling
- Big buttons, quick taps only

---

## 💳 Payment Handling

- App does NOT hold money

- Money goes directly to:
  - Mobile Money
  - Bank

- App only stores:
  - payment status
  - transaction reference

---

## 🔁 Payment Flow

- Merchant can request payment
- Customer pays externally
- System confirms payment
- Debt is marked as paid

---

## 📷 QR Code Idea

- QR should represent a **sale/invoice**, not just a product
- Customer scans → pays → system updates automatically

---

## ⚠️ Stock Reduction Types

- Sold
- Damaged
- Expired
- Personal use
- Adjustment

(Default = Sold)

---

## 🔄 Flexibility

- Merchant can edit mistakes
- Merchant can adjust stock anytime
- System must be forgiving

---

## 🌙 End of Day

- Merchant can review
- Fix errors
- Adjust stock if needed

---

## 🚀 Key Principle

> Let the merchant reduce stock.
> Let the system handle everything else.

---

End.
