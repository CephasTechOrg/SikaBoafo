# Project Description

## Working Name
Ghana SME Business OS

Possible product names:
- MoMoLedger
- MikaOS
- ShopFlow Ghana
- Dwen Ledger
- StorePilot Ghana

## Product Summary
This product is a business operating system for small and medium-sized businesses in Ghana. It is designed for merchants who sell goods daily, manage stock manually or semi-manually, allow trusted customers to buy on credit, and need a faster and more reliable way to collect money, send reminders, and understand how their business is performing.

The system combines:
- sales tracking
- inventory tracking
- customer management
- debt and invoice tracking
- mobile money payment links
- WhatsApp and SMS reminders
- receipts
- basic daily profit and cash visibility
- owner and worker roles
- audit logs and staff accountability

## The Pain We Are Solving

### 1. Revenue leakage
Many small businesses lose money because:
- sales are not recorded properly
- staff forget to record transactions
- stock disappears without explanation
- debt records are scattered across notebooks, chats, and memory
- owners cannot reconcile what was sold versus what was paid

### 2. Poor debt collection
Customers often buy now and pay later. Businesses struggle because:
- they forget who owes them
- reminders are inconsistent
- customers delay payment
- there is no easy digital flow to request and receive payment
- debt collection depends too much on personal follow-up

### 3. Weak inventory visibility
Shops often run out of fast-moving items because:
- stock levels are not updated from actual sales
- low-stock alerts are missing
- damaged or adjusted stock is not tracked properly
- owners do not know what needs restocking early enough

### 4. Low business visibility
Owners often do not know:
- total sales today
- who sold what
- what remains unpaid
- which items are finishing
- whether workers are making suspicious edits or cancellations
- whether the business is truly profitable

### External users

## Core Product Promise
This is not just an inventory app.
This is not just a bookkeeping app.
This is not just a payment app.

This product should feel like:
> The system that helps Ghanaian businesses sell, track stock, recover debts, collect through mobile money, and understand the business every day.

## MVP Product Scope
Version 1 must allow a merchant to:
- create a business
- add workers
- add products
- record sales
- track inventory changes
- create invoices
- track customer balances
- send payment links through WhatsApp or SMS
- verify payment through backend webhook flow
- send receipts and reminders
- view a simple dashboard for sales, debts, and low stock

## Product Principles
- Ghana-first
- mobile money first
- simple language
- low-friction onboarding
- usable by non-technical shop owners
- trust through auditability
- event-driven stock and payment updates
- fast and reliable daily workflows

## What Makes The Product Memorable
The product becomes memorable when it:
- reminds owners who owes them
- lets customers pay quickly with MoMo
- shows who changed stock
- warns when stock is low
- sends daily business summaries
- feels built for Ghana, not imported from another market
